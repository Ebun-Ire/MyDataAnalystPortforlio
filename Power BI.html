Power BI Projects

I used Power BI for analyzing maternal health risks, including data preparation, modeling, and visualization. With Power Query, I cleaned and integrated data, and with Power BI's modeling tools, I created a strong data model and calculated insights using DAX.
Using this, I built interactive dashboards in Power BI for users to explore pregnancy risk trends and insights. I also used DAX for additional insights and predictions, showing relationships between attributes and risk levels.

This project highlights my skills in data preparation, modeling, and visualization with Power BI, showcasing my ability to handle complex healthcare data and present insights effectively through dashboards.

Utilizing Power BI, the project analyzed a food app business to understand customer behavior, engagement, and revenue.
Findings showed 11,768 website visits, 5,112 discounted deals, and $13,380 revenue. Of 2,205 customers, 1,422 were married, 783 single, 639 graduates, and 1,566 non-graduates. Married customers generated $784,666 revenue, while singles generated $450,060.

Analysis of 20 complaints highlighted areas for improvement. Key insights include understanding the website visit-purchase correlation, identifying customer segments, and monitoring satisfaction.

This project demonstrated proficiency in data analysis and visualization using Power BI, aiding informed business decisions for growth in the food app industry.

In this project, I used Power BI to analyze COVID-19 data in the UK, focusing on vaccination progress and death rates over time. The analysis revealed an average of 27.73K people vaccinated per day, totaling 28.33M vaccinations. I also examined variations in death rates to understand the virus's impact.

Visualizations depicted total deaths and first-dose vaccinations by registration date, offering a comprehensive view of COVID-19's cumulative impact and vaccination progress. Additionally, I tracked the second-dose vaccination progress, showing a steady increase over time.
Analyzing monthly death rates provided insights into COVID-19's impact on public health. Overall, Power BI showcased its ability to analyze and visualize COVID-19 data, providing a comprehensive understanding of the situation in the UK.

I used Power BI to analyze Microsoft's stock data from 1986 to 2022, aiming to understand price trends and visualize insights effectively.
With Power Query, I cleaned and shaped the data, while Power BI's modeling capabilities enhanced analysis. Analyzing the data uncovered daily price changes and trading volumes, providing valuable market insights.

Visualizations, including interactive charts and graphs, effectively presented these findings. Leveraging advanced features like DAX calculations, I computed moving averages and percentage changes, deepening understanding of stock performance. Dynamic dashboards facilitated detailed analysis and filtering by time periods.
